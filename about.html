About Us

Mega Q Mart (formerly known as Nepa Q Mart) is an established wet and dry market along the busy highway of EDSA, Cubao that provides quality fresh goods at affordable prices.

Vision

Mega Q Marketing Corp. aims to advance as a major stakeholder in the local community in terms of sourcing and providing fresh market produce through a cost-effective, hassle-free food delivery service and strategic e-commerce marketing.

Mission

Our mission is to offer customers premium goods at mega affordable prices. Order through your phone or the web and get it delivered in the comfort of your own place.Feel safe, at ease, and without stress.

Core Values

Our team values artistry, innovation, attention to detail, decisiveness, responsiveness and most of all a fascination for all things food-related. We employ this attitude to create a customer service experience that is nothing short of excellent.

Fresh and Fast

We use quality ingredients from trusted suppliers to guarantee superb and budget-friendly products. Our crew upholds the importance of customer support and satisfaction. This includes fostering a friendly atmosphere while maintaining a professional character. Fresh merchandise, fast and reliable service
